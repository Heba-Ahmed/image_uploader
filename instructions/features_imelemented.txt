Features Implemented:

- Upload pictures and give them a title and a description.
- List all uploaded pictures and display them in a grid ( and display its title).
- You should allow removal of uploaded pictures.
- Clicking a picture display larger version of the image with its description.
- I tried to make my website look attractive and be user-friendly.
- I tried also to make it responsive; adapting to different screen sizes.


Extras:

- Implement a simple user registration/login system. ( using hashing for saving/retrieving password)
- implement simple user logout.
- Only logged-in users can upload pictures. 
- Allow picture removal by the person who uploaded it.
- Dynamic resizing of pictures in 'List' page ( I divided the screen into 6 equal spaces and not depending on screen size).
